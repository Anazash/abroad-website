ABOUT US

MEDIPOL is focused and dedicated to you. With 24 years experience in English language and relatively long entry into Study Abroad,
we understand your need for international education, as well as work permit and PR requirements. We help you get started with learning English the international way
with our various programs like IELTS, Speak smart etc. To help you achieve your goals for particular universities in preferred countries we encourage you to get the required
scores of 6.5 with a Band no less than 5.5. If you get a total average score of only 5.5 we help place you in colleges and universities in New Zealand
where they are not too particular. On completion of your course you will be eligible for a stay back option for a year or two in most countries and 3 years in Canada,
depending on the course, after which you could apply for your PR or permanent residence.
