General Information

Canada is a country in North America consisting of ten provinces and three territories. Lacated in the northern part of the continent, it extends from the Atlantic to the Pacific and northward into the Artic Ocean. Canada is one of the wealthiest countires in the world with the eighth highest per capita income globally. The capital city is Ottawa, while Toronto is the largest city. The other main cities include Dawson Creek, Winnipeg, Edmonton, Niagara Lake, Abbotsford, including others. The official languages are English and French. The currency is the Canadian dollar $ (CAD). It’s per capital income is $50.577. Its population is 35,540,419 in 2014, while it is one of the largest countries in the world.
